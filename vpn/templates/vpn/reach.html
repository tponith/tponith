How to Reach NIT Hamirpur

Air: ~ 1.5hr drive (Dharamshala Airport)
Air: ~ 3hr drive (Chandigarh Airport)
Rail: ~1.8hr journey (Una Station)
Road: 10min drive (Main Bus Stand)

Via Air

NIT Hamirpur is accessible by air via a comfortable 1.5-hour drive from the modern Dharamshala Airport, which operates regular flights to New Delhi and seamlessly connects further to Chandigarh Airport with a journey duration of approximately 3 hours, ensuring comprehensive national connectivity.

Via Rail

NIT Hamirpur is conveniently reached by rail via the historic Una station in Himachal Pradesh, offering a remarkably scenic journey of approximately 1.8 hours, with frequent, reliable, consistently punctual and safe train services connecting major cities and ensuring smooth, efficient regional travel.

Via Road

The campus is situated at Anu in Hamirpur district of Himachal Pradesh and is a 10min drive from the main bus stand of Hamirpur at Hamirpur-Awah devi road, while the city of Hamirpur is connected with rest of the country by road.
