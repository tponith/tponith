How to Reach NIT Hamirpur

Air Link -

NIT Hamirpur is located with a journey duration of 1hr from Dharamshala Airport which is directly connected to New Delhi Airport through various flights of Air India & Spice Jet, with a flight duration of 1hr.
NIT Hamirpur is also conected to Chandigarh Airport with a beautiful journey duration of nearly 3.3hrs. Chandigarh Airport is connected to most of the airports of the country with variety of flights.

Our vehicles will be available on both the Airports to pick and drop with prior intimation.

Rail Link -

The nearest broad gauge train head is at Una(Himachal Pradesh) which is at a beautiful journey duration of nearly 2.3hrs.

Road Link -

The campus is situated at Anu in Hamirpur district of Himachal Pradesh and is a 10min drive from the main bus stand of Hamirpur at Hamirpur-Awah devi road. The city of Hamirpur is well connected with rest of the country by road.
